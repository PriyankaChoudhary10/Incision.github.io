Membership

The policy will cover you for

01\

Medical professional liability

Cover for claims arising from medical and professional services in your private practice

02\

Professional indemnity

Cover for appraiser and trainer work, libel and slander, loss of documents and breach of professional confidentiality

03\

Public liability

Cover for claims made against you for bodily injury to members of the public

04\

Cyber liability

Cover for claims arising from a cyber event, up to the value of £1m

05\

Commercial legal protection

Cover for defence costs for GMC, disciplinary and coronial hearings or inquiries. Also includes HMRC investigations and a claims review service.
