Claims

Our medico-legal advisors are on-hand 24/7 to assist you in the event of a claim. They are led by Dr. Michael Kyriagis, who holds qualifications in both medicine and law.

The team also provides risk management services in the form of newsletters, articles, seminars and webinars. Where the circumstances require it, they can also provide one-to-one risk management advice for our members.
