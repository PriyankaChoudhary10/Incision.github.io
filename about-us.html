About us

We provide a sharp, refreshing and intelligent approach to medical indemnity insurance. Since 2010, we’ve developed a number of indemnity schemes for specialist surgeons in the UK, providing competitive alternatives to the Medical Defence Organisations.

Formerly known as Isisis, we rebranded to Incision in 2018. Although our name has changed, our team hasn't: with over 50 years’ combined experience in this field, we're always on hand to talk to you. We believe personal contact and service is the cornerstone of our success – and we always will.
